How to Use Career Copilot

  1. Install the Extension: Add Career Copilot to your Chrome browser with a single Click!
  2. Insert your OpenAI Key: Click on the extension and save your key. Open AI API platform.openai.com/api-keys
  3. Enable Closed Captions: To experience the full power of Career Copilot, make sure to enable closed captions during your Google Meetings.
  4. Enable Real-time Analysis: Once installed, Career Copilot will automatically begin analyzing meeting subtitles.
  5. Send Requests to OpenAI: Seamlessly send requests to OpenAI for additional information or answers during your meetings.
